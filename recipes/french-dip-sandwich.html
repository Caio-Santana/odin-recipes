Ingredients

  • 4 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can beef consomme
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 pound thinly sliced deli roast beef
  • 8 slices provolone cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Open hoagie rolls and lay them out on a baking sheet.
  2. Heat beef consomm√© and water in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat to make a rich beef broth. Place roast beef in broth and warm for 3 minutes. Arrange meat on hoagie rolls and top each roll with 2 slices of provolone.
  3. Bake sandwiches in the preheated oven until cheese melts, about 5 minutes. Serve sandwiches with small bowls of warm broth for dipping.
